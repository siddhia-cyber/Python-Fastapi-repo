Artificial Intelligence is transforming modern industries. 
Machine learning algorithms enable systems to learn patterns from data.
Deep learning models such as neural networks are widely used in image and speech recognition.
AI applications include healthcare diagnosis, autonomous vehicles, and recommendation systems.
Information Retrieval focuses on searching and ranking relevant documents.
Search engines use indexing techniques to efficiently retrieve data.
Vector space models and term frequency are commonly used methods.
Relevance scoring helps rank documents based on user queries.
